Hello, Welcome to NeuroCrop

NeuroCrop is a free neural network based selfie cropping site. The modern world is developing rapidly. There are online agreements, contracts and various other documents that use selfie photos. This site is geared towards office, school, university, travel, etc. It allows you to get a photo for documents without leaving your home.
